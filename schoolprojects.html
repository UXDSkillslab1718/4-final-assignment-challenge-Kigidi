Project Bespoke Design

Design thinking journal and a design of a bucket list gift giving experience

In Project Bespoke Design we were required to work in groups of two and create a personalised, crowdfunded bucket list gift unwrapping experience for our users which were students from other group. Besides creating an experience for the user we were also working with a client, which was "Hallmark". Thourght all this time we had to keep design thinking journal where we would write about things learned and our overall progress. By the end we had to present our gift unwrapping experiences, crowdfunding and physical card.
